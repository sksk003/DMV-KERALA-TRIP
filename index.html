Monsoon Risk Mitigation & Backups

Detailed contingency routes and activities mapped out to tackle heavy rainfalls safely.

1. Landslides or Hill Road Closures (Vagamon)

Impact: The winding hill climb to Vagamon is blocked by mud slips.

Backup Plan: Divert the Tempo Traveller via Nedumkandam and Kanjar, which are lower-altitude and less landslide-prone roads. Add 1.5 hours buffer to the Day 2 route timeline.

2. Vagamon Glass Bridge Closed

Impact: High winds or heavy showers cause management to suspend bridge walks.

Backup Plan: Switch the adventure park schedule with a indoor team building session in the resort activity hall (Valley View or Pine Breeze). Alternatively, visit the Kurisumala Ashram indoor museum gardens.

3. Varkala Beach Swimming Restrictions

Impact: Red flags hoisted at Papanasam beach restricting swimming due to high waves.

Backup Plan: Avoid seawater swimming completely. Shift group activities to the resort swimming pool (Krishnatheeram). Redirect seaside walk to Kappil backwaters where water is calm and sheltered.

4. Varkala Paramotoring Cancelled

Impact: Poor wind conditions or rain halts paramotoring operations.

Backup Plan: Replace paramotoring activity with a visit to the historical Anjengo Fort (built in 1696) located 12km south of Varkala cliff. Provides excellent shelter and rich team exploration paths.
